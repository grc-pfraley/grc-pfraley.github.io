About

Founded in 2022, ¡Salud! Rooftop Bar and Lounge is proud to be an energetic environment that aims to facilitate a fun and unique experience while integrating Spanish culture into California's central coast. With live Spanish music and traditional Spanish cocktails, you'll feel like you were transported right into the heart of Barcelona.
