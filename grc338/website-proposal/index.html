Introduction

¡Salud! Rooftop Bar and Lounge

¡Salud! is a Spanish-inspired rooftop bar located in downtown San Luis Obispo, California. With views overlooking the luscious rolling hills and live music on weekends, ¡Salud! is a time you won't want to miss!

Target audience

The bar's target audience includes Cal Poly students looking to get a drink with friends, as well as tourists and SLO residents who favor Spanish drinks and culture.

The user's goals when visiting this site are either to discover more information about the bar, check out the drink menu, view the live music schedule, or to reserve a booth in advance.

About

Founded in 2022, ¡Salud! Rooftop Bar and Lounge is proud to be an energetic environment that aims to facilitate a fun and unique experience while integrating Spanish culture into California's central coast. With live Spanish music and traditional Spanish cocktails, you'll feel like you were transported right into the heart of Barcelona.
